# Code of Conduct — ميثاق السلوك

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in مخلب a respectful, inclusive experience for everyone, regardless of nationality, dialect, sect, gender, age, or experience level.

## Our Standards

### Expected behavior
- Be respectful and kind
- Welcome newcomers
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Respect cultural and religious diversity within the Arab world

### Unacceptable behavior
- Sectarian or political attacks
- Discrimination based on nationality, dialect, or background
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any behavior that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Cultural Sensitivity

مخلب serves Arabic speakers across 22+ countries with diverse cultures, dialects, and traditions. Contributors must:

- Respect all Arabic dialects equally — no dialect is "better" or "more correct"
- Handle Islamic/religious content with care and accuracy
- Avoid political commentary in code, docs, or discussions
- Be mindful that cultural norms vary across the Arab world

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All reports will be reviewed and addressed.
